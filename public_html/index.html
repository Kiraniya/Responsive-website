Our Story

The Rosa, is a business that prepares and serves food and drinks to customers. Meals are generally served and eaten on the premises, but our restaurants also offer take-out and food delivery service. A Boulanger established a business selling soups and other "restaurants" ("restoratives"). Additionally, while not the first establishment where one could order food , or even soups, it is thought to be the first to offer a menu of available choices.

Menu

The restaurant and lounge offerings at The New Yorker Hotel are guaranteed to please even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re looking to satisfy a late-night craving at Tick Tock Diner, indulge in some hearty Tuscan cuisine, or sip a cocktail with friends, we’ve got you covered. TICK TOCK DINER This 24-hour diner is a New York institution, dishing out delicious American-style comfort food – everything from juicy burgers to crisp, fresh salads and an all-day breakfast menu. We’re particularly famous for our sandwiches – treat yourself to a Monte Christo, or a Triple Decker Club.
